    \caption{``foo'' actually has the type ``$\forall a. \forall b. (a\rightarrow b)\rightarrow b\rightarrow b$'', which fails as `b' can only be bound to ``a $\rightarrow$ b'' when ``a'' and ``b'' are equal, not for all ``a'' and ``b''.}
